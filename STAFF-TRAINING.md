# ROSS Tax Prep Staff Training Manual

## Welcome
Welcome to the ROSS Tax Prep Platform! This guide covers all essential workflows, compliance, and security best practices for staff.

---

## 1. Platform Overview
- Secure login with MFA
- Client onboarding and document upload
- IRS e-file workflow (DIY and ERO/PTIN)
- Payment processing and refund tracker
- Admin console and audit log

## 2. Compliance & Security
- Always use strong, unique passwords and enable MFA
- Never share credentials or client data
- Report suspicious activity to compliance@rosstaxprep.com
- Follow IRS Publication 1075 and company privacy policy

## 3. E-File Workflow
- Verify client identity and return data
- Use the e-file dashboard to transmit returns
- Monitor status and acknowledgments
- Handle rejections per IRS guidance

## 4. Payment & Refunds
- Accept payments via Stripe, Square, or bank products
- Track refund status in the client portal
- Escalate payment issues to support@rosstaxprep.com

## 5. Document Management
- Upload, sign, and store documents securely
- Use DocuSign for e-signature workflows
- Retain records per IRS and company policy
