## I. INSTITUTIONAL ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS (34 CFR 600.2)

### Current Status: ❌ NOT ELIGIBLE

Ross Tax Academy does **NOT** meet the eligibility requirements for Title IV financial aid participation because:

#### 1. **Accreditation Requirement** (34 CFR 600.4)
**Requirement:** Institution must be accredited by nationally recognized accrediting agency
- ✅ **SACSCOC**, ✅ **DEAC**, ✅ **HLC**, ✅ **ACBSP**, or similar regional/national body
- **Ross Tax Academy Status:** ❌ NOT ACCREDITED
- **Consequence:** Cannot participate in any Title IV programs

#### 2. **Institutional Control Requirement** (34 CFR 600.5)
**Requirement:** Institution must maintain effective control over instructional content, faculty, and operations
- **Ross Tax Academy Status:** ✅ MET (State-authorized Texas entity)

#### 3. **Governance Requirement** (34 CFR 600.6)
**Requirement:** Institution must be legally authorized to operate in state where located
- **Ross Tax Academy Status:** ✅ MET (Texas Workforce Commission authorization pending)

#### 4. **Operational Requirement** (34 CFR 600.7)
**Requirement:** Institution must have been in continuous operation for minimum of 2 years
- **Ross Tax Academy Status:** ⏳ TIMELINE (Operational since 2025, will meet requirement in 2027)

## III. ALTERNATIVE FINANCING OPTIONS (Compliant Language)

### What Students CAN Use

#### A. **Private Education Loans** (NOT Title IV)
✅ **Allowed:** Students may obtain private loans from non-federal lenders:
- Sallie Mae
- SoFi
- Earnest
- Ascent
- Other private student loan providers

**Disclosure:** "Private student loans are NOT federal loans. Interest rates, terms, and repayment conditions vary by lender. Students should compare rates and terms carefully."

#### B. **Employer Reimbursement**
✅ **Allowed:** Employers may reimburse employees for training costs
- Tuition assistance programs
- Professional development budgets
- Educational stipends
- Section 127 employer education benefits

**Disclosure:** "Ross Tax Academy works with employers to process educational reimbursement. Students should verify their employer's education benefit policies and limitations."

#### C. **Institutional Payment Plans** (0% APR)
✅ **Allowed:** Ross Tax Academy offers in-house payment plans
- 4-6 month installments
- 0% interest
- No credit check required

#### D. **Savings & Out-of-Pocket Payment**
✅ **Allowed:** Students may pay using personal savings

#### E. **529 Education Savings Plans**
✅ **ALLOWED (with limitations):** Students may withdraw from 529 plans
- Qualified education expenses include tuition & fees
- Room & board does NOT qualify (Ross is distance education)
- Tax implications apply if used for non-qualified expenses

**Disclosure:** "Students should consult with a tax professional before withdrawing from 529 plans, as use of funds outside qualified education expenses may result in taxes and penalties."

## IV. PROHIBITED CLAIMS & LANGUAGE

### ❌ CANNOT Say (Title 5 Violation)

| Prohibited Claim | Why It's Illegal | Consequence |
|---|---|---|
| "Eligible for FAFSA" | Only accredited schools | False advertising |
| "We accept federal student loans" | Non-accredited ineligible | Federal enforcement action |
| "Financial aid available" (without context) | Misleading to consumers | Consumer fraud |
| "Accredited by Department of Education" | Only regional bodies accredit | FTC violation |
| "Students qualify for federal assistance" | Not true for non-accredited | False advertising |
| "FAFSA processing assistance" | Implies aid eligibility | Deceptive practice |
| "Partner with accredited institutions for financial aid" | Implies direct aid access | False advertising |

### ✅ CAN Say (Compliant Language)

| Compliant Language | Context |
|---|---|
| "Some students may transfer credits to accredited institutions that participate in Title IV" | If true and documented |
| "We provide transcripts for FAFSA applications to accredited partner schools" | Administrative assistance only |
| "Students may explore alternative financing options including private loans" | Links to legitimate lenders |
| "Employer educational benefits may apply to tuition" | Factual statement |
| "We offer institutional payment plans at 0% APR" | Direct institutional financing |
| "State-authorized but not accredited" | Clear, accurate disclosure |

   - Phone: (512) 463-2100

3. **Contact Department of Education:**
   - Federal Student Aid (FSA): studentaid.gov

   - File complaint regarding authorizations violations
