SECTION 4: Surety Bond Requirement (⚠️ ACTION NEEDED)

Texas Requirement: Surety bond protecting student tuition in case of institutional closure or fraud.

Bond Requirements

Requirement Detail
Bond Type Tuition Recovery Bond (Surety Bond)
Amount $10,000 – $50,000 (based on projected annual tuition)
Issuer Licensed surety company (A.M. Best rating A or higher)
Renewal Annual (must be continuous throughout authorization)
Cost Typically 1-3% of bond amount annually (~$200-$1,500/year)

Surety Bond Certificate (Completed)

Original or certified copy from licensed surety provider showing coverage for tuition recovery.

How to Obtain Surety Bond

Steps:

  1. Contact licensed surety providers (Fidelity & Deposit Co., National Casualty, etc.)
  2. Request quote for tuition recovery bond ($10,000-$50,000)
  3. Provide proof of business registration and financial statements
  4. Complete bond application (5-7 business days typically)
  5. Receive bond certificate; submit copy to TWC with application

  • Completeness Review: 10-15 business days (TWC confirms receipt and completeness)
  • Substantive Review: 30-60 days (evaluates policies, curriculum, faculty, finances)
  • Potential Requests for Additional Information: 5-15 business days (for you to respond)
  • Final Approval Decision: 5-10 business days after submission of additional info
  • Total Process: 2-4 months typical

SECTION 7: Critical Compliance Points (for TWC Review)

These items MUST be in place for approval:

  • Distance Education Disclosure: Clear statement that programs are 100% online and available to out-of-state students (under Texas authorization only)
  • No Title IV: Explicit disclosure that institution does NOT participate in federal financial aid
  • Regular & Substantive Interaction (RSI): Documentation that instructors provide predictable, direct engagement
  • Identity Verification: Statement that institution verifies student identity throughout coursework
  • Academic Integrity: Zero-tolerance conduct policy with documented enforcement procedures
  • Record Retention: Policy for maintaining student records per legal requirements (minimum 7 years)
  • Refund Policy: Clear, Texas-compliant 14-day refund window and pro-rata calculation after
  • No Misrepresentation: No claims of accreditation, licensure eligibility, or financial aid access unless qualified
